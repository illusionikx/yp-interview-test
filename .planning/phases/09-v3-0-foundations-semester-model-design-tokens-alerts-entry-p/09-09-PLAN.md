<objective>
Delete the inline `session('status')` / `session('error')` flash banners from the 11 views that carry
their own, leaving `<x-toast>` as the single renderer.

Purpose: **this plan closes a gap none of the phase's source artifacts caught.** 09-CONTEXT.md,
09-RESEARCH.md and 09-UI-SPEC.md all document the 3 Breeze *sentinel* views as the toast's only
landmine. A grep of `resources/views` finds 11 further views that each render their own
`@if (session('status'))` banner (4 of them an `@if (session('error'))` banner too). With `<x-toast />`
now in `layouts/app.blade.php`, every one of those pages renders each flash **twice, in two different
styles** — which fails UX-02's "one popup/alert style is used throughout" and UX-03's Success Criterion
3 ("raises a toaster in one consistent style"), and re-creates in duplicate the very FIX-03 confusion
the toaster was introduced to remove.

This is not scope creep: it is what UX-03 requires, and it is distinct from the token migration of the
~28 `dark:`-carrying views that 09-CONTEXT.md defers to Phase 14 (FIX-02, UX-05). That deferral is
about *token classes*; this is about *duplicate flash rendering*.
Output: 11 views, banner blocks removed. Turns ToastTest tests 2 and 4 green.
</objective>
