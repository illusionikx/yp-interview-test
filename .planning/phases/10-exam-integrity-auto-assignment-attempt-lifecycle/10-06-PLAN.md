<objective>
Make v2.0's CRITICAL cross-subject leak **structurally unexpressible** (D-1, T-10-01).

Purpose: the live leak vector is `Exam::scopeVisibleTo()`, which walks `exam ‚Üí sections (pivot) ‚Üí
enrollments` and **never checks that `exam.subject_id === section.subject_id`**. Today only the
assignment UI prevented a cross-subject attach ‚Äî and plan 05 just deleted that UI, leaving the pivot a
loaded gun with no trigger guard. This plan removes the gun.

After this plan there is no longer any way to say "exam X is visible to a class of subject Y." Not a
check that could be forgotten ‚Äî an absence of the relationship itself. That is why CLS-05 and INT-04
are settled together, by construction.

This is the phase's atomic core: the migration, the predicate, the relations and the ~16 test fixtures
must land in one plan, because removing the relation makes every unconverted caller a **runtime fatal**
(BadMethodCallException / "Base table or view not found"), not a soft failure.

Output: pivot gone, visibility derived from subject enrollment, `CrossSubjectVisibilityTest` green.
</objective>
