## Phase Goal (User Story)

**As a** lecturer, **I want to** build the full teaching content model — classrooms, subjects, class-subject links, student rosters, and complete exams — **so that** everything is authored and correct before any of it is exposed to a student.

*(Derived from the Phase 2 `**Goal:**` line in `.planning/ROADMAP.md`.)*

**This slice:** *As a lecturer, I want to create, edit, and delete subjects, so that exams have a subject to belong to.*

<objective>
Deliver end-to-end subject management (CLS-02, D-02): a lecturer can list, create, edit, and delete subjects through Breeze-styled Blade forms, gated by the existing Phase-1 `role:lecturer` route group. This is the first working vertical slice of the phase and the simplest resource (single required `name`, optional `code`), establishing the resource-controller + Form Request + Blade pattern every later authoring slice reuses.

Purpose: Exams (02-04) belong to a subject; subjects must exist and be manageable first.
Output: `Lecturer\SubjectController`, Store/UpdateSubjectRequest, three Blade views, resource routes, and `SubjectControllerTest`.
</objective>
