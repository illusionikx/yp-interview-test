<objective>
Deliver the final vertical slice: the student submits, and time actually runs out. A transactional, idempotent submit() finalizes the attempt; the take page gets its live Alpine countdown (ticking, warning/critical color escalation, auto-submit at zero) and the confirm-submit modal. This closes the two-layer auto-submit (D-05): the client countdown auto-POSTs at zero, and the server backstop (already in finalizeIfExpired from 04-02) remains authoritative if the client never fires.

Purpose: TAK-04 (auto-submit on expiry, no further changes accepted) and TAK-02's live countdown driven by the server deadline (D-03/D-09), with an idempotent transactional submit safe under double-click/two-tab races.
Output: AttemptController@submit, the attempts.submit route, the wired confirm modal, and the live countdown + auto-submit transition on the take page.

Slice capability gained: after this plan a student can review and submit with confirmation, watch the clock escalate, and — whether they click Submit, let the clock hit zero, or close the tab — the attempt finalizes exactly once, server-authoritatively.
</objective>
