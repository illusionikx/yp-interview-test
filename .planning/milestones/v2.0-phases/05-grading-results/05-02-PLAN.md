<objective>
The core vertical slice: MCQ answers are auto-graded the moment an attempt finalizes, and a student can view their own gated, accurate result. This wires `AttemptGrader` into the Phase-4 `Attempt::lockAndFinalize()` chokepoint (one hook covers manual submit AND lazy auto-submit), adds an ownership-only `viewResult` policy, and ships the student result screen with its two gated states.

**After this plan, a real student can:** submit an all-MCQ exam and immediately see their scored, per-question result; and, for an exam with open-text, see a calm "awaiting grading" page instead of any premature score.

Purpose: Deliver GRD-01 (auto-grade on submission), GRD-03 (result gated until graded), and GRD-04 (own graded result view) end-to-end for the MCQ/auto path. Implements D-01, D-02, D-03, D-05, D-07, D-08.
Output: AttemptGrader service, hooked finalize, viewResult policy, Student\ResultController, student result route + view.
</objective>
