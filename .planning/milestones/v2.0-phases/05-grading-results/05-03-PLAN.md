<objective>
The lecturer grading vertical slice: a lecturer opens a submitted attempt, sees the per-question breakdown, and grades each open-text answer — and grading the last one flips the attempt to graded and computes its total. This ships the per-attempt drill-in (also the D-06 breakdown view) plus the bounded, mass-assignment-safe grade endpoint that re-runs `AttemptGrader::syncStatus()` under a row lock.

**After this plan, a real lecturer can:** open a student's submitted attempt, score its open-text answers within bounds, and watch the attempt transition to graded (making the student's result visible from 05-02).

Purpose: Deliver GRD-02 (lecturer grades open-text within point value) and close the open-text half of GRD-03 (submitted→graded on the last grade). Implements D-04, D-03, D-06, D-08.
Output: Lecturer\ResultController@show + grading view, GradeAnswerRequest, Lecturer\AnswerGradeController@update, grade + show routes.
</objective>

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| lecturer browser → PATCH /lecturer/results/{attempt}/answers/{answer} | A lecturer-supplied score and route ids cross into an authoritative grade write; bound, target type, and attempt state are enforced server-side. |
| grade-save → AttemptGrader::syncStatus | The completeness/graded transition is decided under a row lock, not from a client signal. |
